Fire


The psychological benefit of fire is undeniable. Knowing that you can creat a fire from next to no tools is very reassuring in many situations. Think about the feeling of relaxtion just sitting and staring into a fire. The satisfying warmth on cold night. The taste of grilled food.
The benefits of fire in survival are undeniable and it is one of the first things covered in most courses.
Fire can also be used for signals, hardening tools, drying clothes and other things too.
