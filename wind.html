Depending on where you are your requirements for shelter will vary. But whether its the hot deserts of the Namib or the icy tundra of Alaska it is certain that you will need some kind of shelter. Shelter can be as basic as a hat to keep the sun off your head, or as advanced as stacking logs to build a wall, sealing walls with mud or clay and working out a chimney to draw out smoke and prevent asphyxiation.
Also included in this page is navigation. How to find out where you are, where you want to go and how to stay on course. We will be trying different types of shelters, tarps and knots. Creating simples compass and more complicated compass. Exploring the basics of navigation.
